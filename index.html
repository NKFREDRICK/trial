Welcome to Taita Taveta National Polytechnic Library

Established in 2009, Prof. Justin Irina Library serves as a vital role in assisting the academic endeavours of students, research scholars and faculty. The library offers access to books, online databases, ebooks, and other digital resources. It provides tranquil spaces for individual study and group discussions (Gazebos) fostering an atmosphere conducive for focussed learning.

Prof. Justin Irina Library offers the following services to the academic community

  • Circulation Services
  • Reference Services
  • Current Awareness Services
  • E-learning & Digital Resources Services
  • Reprographic Services
  • Information Literacy Services

    • The Library is a member of the Kenya Libraries & Information Services Consortium (KLISC). As a member of KLISC the TTNP Library provides its users access to several subscribed full text online Databases and E-books such as BUKU.

    The Library has automated the activities using the library Automation Package ABN
